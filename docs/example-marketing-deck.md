# Sensai Example Marketing Deck (19 Slides)

## Slide 1: Cover
**Title:** Sensai  
**Subtitle:** AI-powered market and competitor intelligence for faster commercial decisions  
**Footer:** Confidential | Month Year

## Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline:** Companies are data-rich but insight-poor  
- Critical market signals are public, but fragmented and underused  
- Strategy and marketing teams are stretched  
- Traditional research is expensive and too slow for fast-moving markets  
- Decisions get delayed or made with partial evidence

## Slide 3: Why Now
**Headline:** Market speed has increased. Decision cycles have not.  
- Competitor moves are faster and more frequent  
- Leadership expects evidence-backed decisions in real time  
- AI now makes large-scale signal processing practical and affordable  
- Firms need strategic insight without building full in-house capability

## Slide 4: Our Solution
**Headline:** Sensai turns public data into decision-ready intelligence  
- AI-powered signal collection and synthesis  
- Advanced analytics to find what matters  
- Visual, executive-ready outputs  
- Clear commercial recommendations, not raw data summaries

## Slide 5: What You Get
**Headline:** Outputs designed for action  
- Market and competitor landscape snapshots  
- Threat/opportunity alerts  
- Strategic implications by customer segment or market  
- Recommendation pack: "what this means" and "what to do next"

## Slide 6: How We Work
**Headline:** Human + AI model for speed and trust

2. Collect and structure relevant public signals  
3. Analyze trends, moves, and implications  
4. Apply analyst quality review  
5. Deliver action plan and leadership-ready narrative

**Headline:** Better decisions, lower cost, faster turnaround
