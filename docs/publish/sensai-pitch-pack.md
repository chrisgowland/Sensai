# Sensai Pitch Pack

## Page 1 - Executive Summary
Sensai helps commercial teams turn public data into decision-ready market and competitor intelligence.  
We combine AI speed with commercial interpretation to deliver commercial value through faster, better-informed decisions at lower cost than traditional research models.

## Page 2 - Our Mission
- We are deeply commercial.
- We leverage cutting-edge AI, analytics, and data to improve the performance of your business.
- We do not produce conceptual PowerPoint; we produce directly actionable insight at a fraction of the cost of internal teams.
- We build tools and processes to improve your business.
- We move 10-100x faster than you can alone.

## Page 3 - Problem
- Valuable market and competitor signals are publicly available but fragmented and underused.
- Internal teams often lack specialist analytics capacity.
- Traditional insight models are expensive and slow.
- Outputs frequently describe trends but fail to drive decisions.
- Insight is often generated as single-topic outputs, with a heavy management tax to connect findings and make coherent decisions.
- Many teams still operate with traditional methods and have not adapted to the step change in what modern data and AI can deliver.

## Page 4 - Solution
Sensai delivers:
- AI-powered signal capture and synthesis
- Advanced analytics and prioritization
- Executive-ready visualization and narrative
- Clear "so what" recommendations tied to commercial outcomes

## Page 5 - Core Use Cases
1. Competitor price scraping to support price optimization and margin protection.
2. Professional services website/profile reviews to improve engagement and conversion.
3. Automated competitor monitoring to replace expensive manual internal/external research.
4. Automated competitor/regulator monitoring with rapid "so what" interpretation.
5. Online purchase journey reviews to reduce friction and improve conversion.
6. Website image quality audits to improve trust and brand perception.
7. Competitor feature/proposition reviews to sharpen differentiation and roadmap choices.
8. Automated social review management insights to improve retention and reputation.

## Page 6 - Differentiation
- Commercial-first: decisions, not data dumps.
- Not an insight firm; we are wired for commercial impact.
- Human + AI quality model: faster outputs with analyst validation.
- Flexible delivery model: project-based and recurring.
- Built for strategy/marketing/commercial leaders.
