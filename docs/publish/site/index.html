Page 1 - Executive Summary

Sensai helps commercial teams turn public data into decision-ready market and competitor intelligence.

  • We are deeply commercial.

  • We build tools and processes to improve your business.
  • We move 10-100x faster than teams can alone.

Page 3 - Problem

  • Valuable market and competitor signals are publicly available but fragmented and underused.
  • Internal teams often lack specialist analytics capacity.
  • Traditional insight models are expensive and slow.
  • Outputs often describe trends but fail to drive decisions.
  • Insight is frequently single-topic; management carries the integration burden.
  • Many teams have not adapted to the step change in data and AI capability.

Page 4 - Solution

  • AI-powered signal capture and synthesis
  • Advanced analytics and prioritization
  • Executive-ready visualization and narrative
  • Clear "so what" recommendations tied to commercial outcomes

Page 5 - Core Use Cases

  1. Competitor price scraping for price optimization and margin protection.
  2. Website/profile reviews to improve engagement and conversion.
  3. Automated competitor insight vs expensive manual research.
  4. Automated industry/regulatory tracking with "so what" interpretation.
  5. Online journey reviews to reduce friction and improve conversion.
  6. Website image and quality audits for trust and brand performance.
  7. Automated competitor feature and proposition reviews.
  8. Automated social media and review insight management.

Page 6 - Differentiation

  • Commercial-first: decisions, not data dumps.

  • Human + AI quality model with analyst validation.
  • Flexible delivery model: project-based and recurring.
