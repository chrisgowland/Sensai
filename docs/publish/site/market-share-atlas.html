Segment Source What It Reports Release Cadence
Private healthcare PHIN Consultant/hospital/procedure measures, quality and activity, geography-enabled analysis. Quarterly updates (12-month rolling period).
Retail banking products BoE Bankstats Lending/deposit product structure and market-level aggregates. Monthly.
Mortgage market FCA Mortgage Lending Statistics Gross advances, commitments, balances, LTV and affordability distributions. Quarterly.
Mortgage geography FCA Mortgage PSD Geography Regional/national mortgage product sales and characteristics. Quarterly and monthly dashboard extracts.

Telecom market structure Ofcom Communications Market Sector-wide communication market indicators and downloadable data. Annual.

Online platform usage Ofcom Online Nation Platform/service usage and behaviour in UK digital markets. Annual.
Aviation market share CAA UK Airport Data Passenger and traffic data by airport and route contexts. Monthly and annual.

Priority Build Recommendations

  1. Launch with six rich-data verticals: private healthcare, mortgages, energy, telecoms/digital, transport, grocery.
  2. Standardise schema fields: market, submarket, entity, geography, period, value, share, source URL, release date.
  3. Automate by cadence class: monthly, quarterly, annual pipelines with source-level QA checks.
  4. Use PHIN-style granularity as the model for provider/procedure/geographic intelligence products.
