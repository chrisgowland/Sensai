# Sensai Proposition v1

## 1) Positioning Statement
Sensai helps commercial teams make better strategic decisions by turning public data into decision-ready market and competitor intelligence, at a fraction of traditional research cost.

## 2) Ideal Customer Profile (Beachhead)
- Type: Mid-market B2B firms (scale-up to enterprise) with growth targets and lean insight teams
- Functions: Marketing, Strategy, Commercial, Product Marketing
- Buyer roles: Strategy Director, Head of Marketing, Commercial Director
- Trigger events: New market entry, increased competition, pricing pressure, board demand for evidence

## 3) Core Jobs To Be Done
- Understand where the market is moving and where to play
- Track competitor moves and likely implications
- Convert large volumes of public information into clear commercial actions
- Produce board-ready insight faster than internal teams can

## 4) Problem We Solve
- Public data exists but is fragmented, noisy, and underused
- Internal teams lack capacity or specialist analytics skills
- Traditional research is expensive and slow
- Outputs are often descriptive, not decision-oriented

## 5) Value Proposition
### Functional Value
- Faster insight cycles (days, not weeks)
- Lower total cost than equivalent analyst-heavy approaches
- Consistent, repeatable insight process

### Commercial Value
- Better prioritization of markets, segments, and competitors
- Earlier detection of threats/opportunities
- Higher confidence in strategic decisions

### Operational Value
- Extends existing teams without hiring full internal capability
- Flexible service model: project or ongoing subscription

## 6) Offer Structure
### Offer A: Insight Sprint (One-off)
- Use case: Specific decision (e.g., market entry, competitor response)
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Output: Executive summary, evidence pack, action recommendations

### Offer B: Always-On Intelligence (Subscription)
- Use case: Continuous competitor and market monitoring
- Frequency: Monthly updates + quarterly strategic deep dive
- Output: Alerting, trend tracking, recommendation cadence

### Offer C: Strategic Intelligence Partner (Premium)
- Use case: Mission-critical or board-level strategy programs
- Includes: Bespoke analyses, leadership workshops, priority support

## 7) Differentiators
- Commercial-first lens: advice tied to business decisions
- Human + AI model: speed from AI, quality from analyst review
- Public-data mastery: unlocks value from already-available signals
- Decision-ready outputs: narrative and visuals built for executives

## 8) Commercial Use-Case Library (Signal -> Insight -> Value)
### 8.1 Competitor Price Scraping for National Pricing Teams
- Signal: Competitor prices by product, region, and promo cadence
- Insight: Price gaps, discount behavior, and likely elasticity pressure points
- Commercial value: Informs price optimization to protect margin and recover share

### 8.2 Professional Services Website/Profile Review
- Signal: Firm websites, team profiles, proposition wording, and proof points
- Insight: Which messages are driving engagement and where trust gaps exist
- Commercial value: Improves positioning and conversion from prospect visits to enquiry

### 8.3 Automated Competitor Intelligence vs Manual Research
- Signal: Public competitor announcements, content updates, and offer changes
- Insight: Continuous view of competitor strategy shifts without periodic manual studies
- Commercial value: Reduces insight costs and improves speed of strategic response

### 8.4 Industry and Regulatory Action Monitoring
- Signal: Competitor moves, regulator updates, and sector-level events
- Insight: "So what" implications by customer segment, proposition, and channel
- Commercial value: Enables earlier action on risk and faster capture of opportunities

### 8.5 Online Journey Review (Purchase Path Clarity)
- Signal: End-to-end user steps from awareness to purchase/contact
- Insight: Friction points, drop-off risks, and unclear decision moments
- Commercial value: Higher conversion through targeted journey fixes

### 8.6 Website Image and Creative Quality Audit
- Signal: Visual quality, consistency, and relevance across key pages
- Insight: Where visual execution undermines credibility or value perception
- Commercial value: Stronger brand trust and improved engagement quality

### 8.7 Automated Competitor Feature and Proposition Reviews
- Signal: Competitor feature sets, packaging, and value claims over time
- Insight: Gaps in your offer and areas for differentiation
- Commercial value: Better roadmap and go-to-market choices to win in priority segments

### 8.8 Automated Social Media Review Management
- Signal: Reviews, sentiment, recurring complaints, and response quality
- Insight: Root causes of dissatisfaction and topics driving advocacy
- Commercial value: Improves retention, reputation, and lead conversion confidence
